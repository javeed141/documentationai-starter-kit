Available views for people data:

* [Default view](https://java.documentationai.com/docs/people_all)
* [Creating a custom people view](https://java.documentationai.com/docs/creating-a-custom-people-view)
* [Create & save a custom people view](https://java.documentationai.com/docs/create-save-a-custom-people-view)
